Generalized, permissionless and trustless
Content Creation protocol



A platform disintermediating the media industries!


Welcome netizens! This protocol is meant to empower you as a content creator, collector, curator, investor, producer, and anything you can think of in between.

We aim at making the participants to the information economy to interact directly and freely again.

Anyone can choose to paticipate as an investor, a content provider or just to access contents created through this decentralized, peer-to-peer protocol. We are focusing on introducing trustless aspects and an economics design that gets rid of the data trackers, and go away from the advertisements business model, using DeFi instead of you as a lab rat whose behavior is the target ot the... well you know them :)

You can now collaborate in creating rich content productions, without having to trust a single source of content, or being dependant of one platform having control over everything.

We will refine the protocol over time so you can define more and more your own rules of interaction around any content you want to see created!
